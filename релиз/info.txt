Short description: Gentle break reminders for water, posture, eyes and stretches. Offline, no signup.
Long description:

Standy got your back. Literally. It nudges you to take healthy micro-breaks while you work at a desk - without accounts, ads or subscriptions.

Set how often you want a nudge and Standy rotates through stand-up, drink-water, eye-rest and posture-check reminders. When one fires you can mark it Done, Snooze it, or Skip. Open a short guided stretch with an animated countdown, then track your progress: a daily goal ring, completed-vs-skipped bars, a streak counter and a GitHub-style activity heatmap.

Everything is stored locally on your device. No login. No email. No paywalls.

- Customizable reminder interval with smart rotation
- Stand up, drink water, eye rest and posture reminders
- Quiet hours and on-demand Do Not Disturb
- Exercise library with animated hold timers
- Daily goal ring and today progress
- Streaks, weekly bars and activity heatmap
- Optional name and photo, no account needed
- Works fully offline
